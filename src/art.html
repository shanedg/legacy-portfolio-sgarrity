words

    scratch

    locate yourself

    extant,

    ecstatic in the gale

    a fast-budding shoot on the human plant sprouting

    from the dirt of the earth made mud

    at the boundary between staid and slippery

    at this curious, unfixed point

    between moments that have vanished

    and moments that have not arrived

    as a body composite

    as an event

    as a party

    as a notebook first half

    all scratch and loose lines

    last half hand-wringing

    over how careless was the first
